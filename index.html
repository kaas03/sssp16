About Our School

Welcome to Shree Secondary School, Pathara Budharam. Located in Nepal, Madhesh Pradesh, Rautahat, Maulapur Municipality Ward 06, our institution was established in 2014 BS. With a rich history of academic excellence, we have been offering a Diploma in Computer Science since 2073 BS.

Our mission is to provide quality education that empowers students to become responsible citizens and future leaders. We focus on holistic development through academic rigor, extracurricular activities, and character building.
